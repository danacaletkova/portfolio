Dana Caletkova

front-end developer based in Perth, WA

I am originally from the Czech Republic where I got a degree in language teaching. During my short teaching career, however, I realised that languages captivate me more than checking attendance or grading exams.

In search of a fresh start, I travelled to Australia where, unexpectedly, an intended year of backpacking stretched into several years of farming. I am always up for a challenge and I enjoyed learning all the new things but eventually felt the nudge to move on.

Out of curiosity, I enrolled in an online front-end development course which unlocked a whole new world for me. I found it so fascinating that I decided to dive in headfirst and study it full-time. I love pushing my boundaries on a daily basis, solving problems, absorbing new knowledge; and I am eager to explore where this path leads.

Beyond coding, I enjoy reading books and spending time in the nature. You could spot me on a hiking trail, at a remote campsite, or at the starting line of one of the next Perth half marathons.
